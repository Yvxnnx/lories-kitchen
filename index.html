About Lorie´s Kitchen

Tradition Since 2000

Lorie´s Kitchen is a homemade food using her own ingridents. Her ability to be creative, and the sense of satisfaction knowing she can provide for herself. The joy when her family tries a new dish she cook and they enjoy it.

Our menu

Pork-BBQ
